About the brand <\/h2>\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\t

Nataliya Novitska<\/p>\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Nataliya Novitska is a womenswear brand, where timeless classics and modern femininity are embodied in suits and dresses. With a focus on exceptional fabrics and a flawless fit, it offers nothing extra: classy yet one-of-a-kind pieces to match any occasion.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

As a reflection of the transformations in the fashion industry, we support sustainability and a rational approach to production. All collections are crafted on demand, using only eco-friendly fabrics made of natural fibers. The brand\u2019s first campaign was shot by Stephan Lisowski starring a top-model Kateryna Zub, who participated in campaigns and walked for Chloe, C\u00e9line, Alexander McQueen, Jil Sander, Chanel, and Dolce&Gabbana.\u00a0<\/p>

The brand Nataliya Novitska was founded by a stylist and designer Nataliya Novitska in Lviv, Ukraine, in 2020. Nataliya made her way in the industry by working as a personal stylist in London, along with styling fashion campaigns in Ukraine, obtaining more than 5 years of experience in fashion. The design path began with creating collections for a Lviv-based brand for several seasons, which eventually inspired Nataliya to set up her own label to sustain the demands of modern women.<\/p><\/div>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\u201cMy taste was mainly formed under the influence of my grandfather, who used to wear suits and hats. Back then, I didn\u2019t realize his claims that a suit makes you look really in-trend. He taught me that trends come and go, but classics and style are permanent. A good suit best reflects the woman of today: she can be independent and strong while remaining tender and sensitive at the same time\u201d.<\/p>

For the time being, the brand was highlighted by Vogue, Harper\u2019s Bazaar, Wonderzine, The Village, HD Fashion, and other Ukrainian press. There are many Ukrainian celebrities among the brand\u2019s admirers, like Ruslan Baginsky and Jamala.<\/p><\/div>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
